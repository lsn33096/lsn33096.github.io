Brief Bio. I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Rutgers University, conducting research in Computer Vision and Deep Learning advised by Board of Governors Professor Dimitris N. Metaxas. Prior to this, I completed my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at UC Irvine with a specialization in Intelligent Systems.

My current research interests lie primarily in (1) diffusion models for complex scene image understanding / generation (e.g. improving T2I diffusion models' capability for text generation), (2) language-guided semantic / instance / panoptic segmentation (e.g. under multi-dataset or multi-modality settings).
