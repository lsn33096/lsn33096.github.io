Qilong Zhangli
Ph.D. student @ Rutgers | qilongzhangli@gmail.com

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Rutgers University, conducting research in Computer Vision and Deep Learning advised by Professor Dimitris Metaxas. Prior to this, I completed my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at UC Irvine with a specialization in Intelligent Systems.

My current research interests primarily lie in instance segmentation and vision-language models with their potential usage in object segmentation and medical imaging.
